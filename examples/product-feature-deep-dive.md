## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I set up milestones for my program?
Create milestone templates when building your course. Define what qualifies as completion (assignment submission, quiz score, manual approval). Students see milestones in their dashboard automatically.

### Can students track multiple goals simultaneously?
Yes. Students can have multiple active goals, each with its own milestones and habits. Perfect for comprehensive programs covering multiple skill areas.

### Does this work for 1-on-1 coaching or just group programs?
Both. Set individual milestones for private clients or use templates for group cohorts. The system adapts to your coaching model.

### What happens if a student falls behind?
You'll get automated alerts based on inactivity thresholds you set. Send personalized check-ins, offer catch-up resources, or schedule intervention calls—all triggered by real data.

### Can I export student progress reports?
Absolutely. Export individual student reports (great for 1-on-1 reviews) or cohort summaries (perfect for stakeholder updates). CSV, PDF, or visual dashboards.

### Is there a mobile app for students to track on-the-go?
Yes. Students can log habits, check milestones, and view progress from native iOS and Android apps. Everything syncs in real-time.
